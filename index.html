The World of Studio Ghilbi!

Studio Ghibli is an animation studio founded in Japan by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata. The films of Hayao Miyazaki are some of the most popular in Japan and the rest of the world. Worldbuilding is a daunting task for animators because every visual element must be created from scratch. There’s nothing there until it’s drawn. This is where Ghibli excels. Their animators demonstrate a mastery of technique and a strict attention to detail with every scene. No matter how farfetched and imaginary, the worlds of Ghibli films consistently feel tactile and realistic.

Each scene is a perfectly crafted masterpiece, like a painting thats telling a story before you. Each scene is full of detail and vivid color, making it feel like you’re watching a painting come to life. The atmosphere of the movies alone are enough to get you immersed into the world of the film.

The soundtracks of these films are always captivating, and not just musical songs. You may find yourself happily humming the heart-warming tunes well after the movie has ended. They work perfectly to compliment the atmosphere and emotions of the film, you can't help but be moved by these rhythmic melodies.

Although not every movie ends in a happily ever after, the art of studio ghibli is such a refreshing escape from the normalities of many movies. These films show that a happy ending isn't needed to create a beautiful story. They don't need to present perfect characters in a perfect life to pull at your heartstrings.
